ABOUT EXTREME FOODS

Extreme Food is a food and recipes website owned by Extreme Group. A venture that focuses on user generated content, videos, articles and recipes from all over the world, Extreme Food carries the legacy of The Extreme of India in content and quality. The website has around 25 recipes with more being added daily. It is a one stop destination for every foodie who is looking for latest food news, recipes based on various cuisines, dietary needs, ingredients, occasions and categories and videos teeming with engaging content.

With its ever growing readership, Extreme Food already has over five million monthly unique users . From step by step recipes to visually appealing images, Extreme food has everything a foodie is looking for. Features on latest food trends, food news, kitchen hacks and tips and videos that make recipes easy even for someone who is not a pro in the kitchen, make this website stand out. Based on calorie count, time taken in preparing a dish, ingredients and dietary requirements, you can make your own diet chart or weekly menu plan.

Anyone can become a blogger with Extreme Food by making a profile and uploading recipes and it is free! Also, it has a vast repository of blogger and chef recipes. Available on both Android and ios, you can download the app and explore this world of culinary fantasy!
