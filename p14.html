Ingredients
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (potatoes, cauliflower, peas, carrots), chopped
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1/2 bell pepper (capsicum), finely chopped
  • 4-5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1/2 cup boiled and mashed green peas
  • 2 tablespoons oil or butter
  • 2 tablespoons pav bhaji masala (available at Indian stores)
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water, as needed
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Lemon wedges
  • 8 pav (soft dinner rolls)
  • Butter, as needed for toasting
Instructions
  1. Boil the mixed vegetables until they are soft and fully cooked. You can use a pressure cooker or a regular pot for boiling. Drain any excess water and set the vegetables aside.
  2. Heat oil or butter in a large pan or griddle (tava) over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  4. Add chopped onions and green chilies. Sauté until the onions become translucent.
  5. Add chopped bell pepper (capsicum) and cook for a few more minutes until it softens.
  6. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and start to break down.
  7. Add pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few more minutes, allowing the spices to blend with the vegetables.
  8. Add the boiled and mashed green peas and the cooked mixed vegetables. Mix everything well and mash the vegetables using a potato masher or the back of a spoon.
  9. Add water to achieve your desired consistency. The bhaji should be thick and have a slightly lumpy texture.
  10. Simmer the bhaji for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Adjust the seasoning if needed.
  11. Garnish the bhaji with freshly chopped coriander leaves and a squeeze of lemon juice. Set it aside.
Enjoy your homemade Pav Bhaji, a delicious and flavorful Indian street food favorite!
