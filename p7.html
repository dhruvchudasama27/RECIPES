Ingredients
  • 12-16 ladyfinger biscuits (savoiardi)
  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 2 tablespoons coffee liqueur (e.g., Kahl√∫a), optional
  • 8 oz (225g) mascarpone cheese
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Cocoa powder, for dusting
  • Chocolate shavings or grated chocolate
  • Fresh berries (strawberries, raspberries), for garnish
  • Mint leaves, for garnish
Instructions
  1. In a mixing bowl, whip the heavy whipping cream until stiff peaks form. Set aside.
  2. In another bowl, combine the mascarpone cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  3. Gently fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture until well combined. This creates a luscious mascarpone filling.
  4. In a shallow dish, combine the strong brewed coffee and coffee liqueur (if using). Dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture briefly, ensuring they are coated but not soggy.
  5. In serving glasses or dessert cups, layer the tiramisu parfait:
    • Start with a layer of coffee-soaked ladyfingers.
    • Add a layer of mascarpone filling on top of the ladyfingers.
    • Repeat the layers until you've used up the ingredients, ending with a layer of mascarpone filling on top.
  6. Chill the assembled parfaits in the refrigerator for at least 2-3 hours, or until the flavors meld together and the dessert sets.
  7. Before serving, dust the tops of the parfaits with cocoa powder for a classic tiramisu look.
  8. Garnish with chocolate shavings or grated chocolate on top of the cocoa powder.
  9. Add a few fresh berries (such as strawberries or raspberries) on the side or on top for a burst of color and flavor.
  10. Optionally, place a mint leaf or two on each parfait for a touch of freshness.
  11. Serve the tiramisu parfaits chilled and enjoy the creamy, coffee-infused layers.
Enjoy your flavorful Decadent tiramisu parfait dessert!
